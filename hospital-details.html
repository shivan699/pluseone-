About the Hospital

St. Metropolitan Medical Center is one of Delhi's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals, serving over 5 lakh patients annually since 1998. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology across 40+ specialties, our 350+ expert doctors deliver compassionate, evidence-based care under one roof.

Our hospital features a 24-hour emergency department, advanced robotic surgery theatre, NABL-accredited diagnostic labs, Level III ICU, and a dedicated mother & child centre — all designed to provide holistic, world-class treatment.
